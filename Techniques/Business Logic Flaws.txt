1️⃣ Information

সংজ্ঞা:
Business Logic Flaws হলো ওয়েব অ্যাপ্লিকেশনের লজিক্যাল বা ডিজাইন ত্রুটি ব্যবহার করে আক্রমণ করা। এখানে আক্রমণকারী সিস্টেমের intended workflow ভেঙে, unauthorized actions বা সুবিধা পেতে পারে।

বৈশিষ্ট্য:

লক্ষ্য: E-commerce, Banking, SaaS, Subscription systems

দুর্বলতা: Validation bypass, workflow abuse, improper access control

উদ্দেশ্য: Unauthorized transactions, privilege escalation, lab-only exploitation

গবেষণার জন্য ব্যবহার: lab simulation, penetration testing, security research


ধাপসমূহ (Attack Lifecycle – গবেষণার দৃষ্টিকোণ)

1. Reconnaissance – Identify workflows and critical business rules


2. Flaw Identification – lab simulation to find rule bypasses


3. Exploitation – Execute actions violating lab-only business logic


4. Actions on Objectives – Unauthorized transactions, lab data access


5. Post-Exploitation – Log successful bypasses and impact analysis



টেকনিক:

Parameter tampering (e.g., price, quantity)

Workflow bypass (e.g., skip approval steps)

Forced browsing (access unauthorized pages in lab)

Race conditions / concurrency simulation

Lab-only privilege escalation or logic abuse

2️⃣ Method

Lab Setup:

VirtualBox / VMware – isolated lab VM

Kali Linux / Ubuntu VM

Web application sandbox (DVWA, WebGoat, custom business logic app)

Isolated network for lab testing


Business Logic Flaw Simulation:

Map lab application workflow

Identify critical parameters and validation points

Manipulate input parameters or URLs to bypass lab workflow

Log unauthorized lab actions and measure exploit effectiveness


Data Collection & Analysis:

Exploit success metrics

Unauthorized transaction simulation logs

Statistical analysis: frequency of bypass, impact on lab data

Visualization: workflow diagram, exploited points


Ethical / Legal Considerations:

All applications and workflows are lab-simulated

Licensed/open-source tools only

Sandbox / isolated network
