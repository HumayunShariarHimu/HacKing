1️⃣ তথ্য (Information)

সংজ্ঞা:
Honeytrap Attack হলো এমন একটি social engineering আক্রমণ যেখানে attacker লোভনীয় persona বা profile তৈরি করে ভিকটিমকে প্রলুব্ধ করে confidential information, credentials বা sensitive data দেয়ার জন্য।

বৈশিষ্ট্য:

লক্ষ্য: Individuals, employees, বা organization users

দুর্বলতা: Human curiosity, attraction, lack of verification

উদ্দেশ্য: Lab-only social engineering research, behavioral analysis

গবেষণার জন্য ব্যবহার: Security awareness, honeypot simulation


Attack Lifecycle:

1. Reconnaissance – Target user বা group behavior চিহ্নিত করা


2. Honeytrap Creation – Lab-safe persona বা profile তৈরি করা


3. Engagement – Lab environment-এ persona interaction চালানো


4. Information Extraction – Lab-safe sensitive data collection


5. Logging & Analysis – Interaction capture & behavioral analysis


6. Awareness & Mitigation – Lab results ব্যবহার করে social engineering awareness তৈরি



টেকনিক:

Fake social media profiles বা lab-safe dating profiles

Lab-only chat, email, বা messaging interactions

Prompts or requests to share confidential lab data

Behavioral logging of engagement & responses



---

2️⃣ পদ্ধতি (Method)

Lab Setup:

Virtual lab environment with simulated users

Snapshot-enabled VMs for rollback

Simulated communication channels: Email, chat, social media simulators

Logging and monitoring tools: Screen capture, chat logs

Scripting: Python, Node.js, or JavaScript for automated lab interactions


Honeytrap Simulation:

Create lab-safe honeytrap persona

Engage simulated targets in lab environment

Encourage lab targets to share information (credentials or lab-only data)

Capture responses and interaction patterns

Evaluate human behavioral tendencies and awareness effectiveness


Data Collection & Analysis:

Metrics: Number of lab targets interacting, information disclosed, engagement duration

Analyze behavioral patterns and trust exploitation

Visualize attack flow: honeytrap creation → victim engagement → data disclosure → logging


Ethical / Legal Considerations:

Lab-only experiments; no real users targeted

Use only simulated accounts and lab-safe data

Conduct all tests in isolated lab environment

3️⃣ টুলস (Tools)

কম্পিউটার টুলস:

Social Engineering Toolkit (SET) – Lab-only persona simulation

Burp Suite / OWASP ZAP – Lab portal simulation

Email clients & local SMTP server – Lab email simulations


অ্যান্ড্রয়েড / মোবাইল টুলস:

Termux – Lab scripting & automation

Android Emulator – Lab testing of messaging apps


সফটওয়্যার প্যাকেজ:

Python: smtplib, requests – Automated lab interaction scripting

Node.js / JavaScript – Lab portal & chat simulation scripting

SQLite / DB tools – Logging & analysis


হার্ডওয়্যার / ল্যাব সেটআপ:

Lab PC or server for running simulations & VMs

Isolated lab network for communication simulations

Optional VoIP or messaging simulators for lab-only engagement
