1️⃣ তথ্য (Information)

সংজ্ঞা:
Whaling Attack হলো একটি স্পেশালাইজড phishing আক্রমণ যা উচ্চপদস্থ কর্মকর্তা (executives, CEOs, CFOs) বা decision-makers-কে টার্গেট করে। মূল লক্ষ্য হলো sensitive corporate data, financial credentials বা confidential information চুরি করা।

বৈশিষ্ট্য:

লক্ষ্য: Executives, board members, high-profile employees

দুর্বলতা: Human trust, urgency response, lack of verification

উদ্দেশ্য: Lab-only phishing research, executive security awareness

গবেষণার জন্য ব্যবহার: Corporate cybersecurity awareness, social engineering studies


Attack Lifecycle:

1. Reconnaissance – Target executive ও তাদের যোগাযোগ পদ্ধতি চিহ্নিত করা


2. Crafting a Targeted Email/Message – Lab-safe urgent, credible-looking message তৈরি করা


3. Delivery – Lab-only email or messaging channel ব্যবহার করে পাঠানো


4. Engagement – Target (lab simulation) click বা response দেওয়ার জন্য প্রলুব্ধ করা


5. Data Collection – Lab-safe credential, document, or info capture


6. Analysis – Interaction patterns ও exploit effectiveness logging



টেকনিক:

Spear phishing specifically tailored to executive interests

Lab-only urgent financial requests or document links

Social engineering messages appearing as CEO/Manager communications

Behavioral logging in lab environment



---

2️⃣ পদ্ধতি (Method)

Lab Setup:

Virtual lab environment with simulated executive accounts

Snapshot-enabled VMs for safe rollback

Lab email server / simulated messaging apps

Logging tools: Screen capture, email response logging

Scripting: Python, Bash, Node.js for automated lab campaigns


Whaling Simulation:

Create lab-safe executive persona

Develop lab-targeted messages with urgency or business context

Deliver to simulated executive accounts

Monitor lab-only interactions and capture responses

Evaluate effectiveness and human factor vulnerabilities


Data Collection & Analysis:

Metrics: Engagement rate, information disclosure, response time

Analyze behavioral patterns and social engineering susceptibility

Visualize attack flow: reconnaissance → targeted message → lab interaction → data capture → logging


Ethical / Legal Considerations:

Lab-only experiments; no real executives targeted

Use only simulated accounts and lab-safe data

Conduct all tests in isolated lab environment

কম্পিউটার টুলস:

Social Engineering Toolkit (SET) – Lab-only whaling campaigns

Burp Suite / OWASP ZAP – Lab phishing portal simulation

Lab email server – Simulated delivery & logging


অ্যান্ড্রয়েড / মোবাইল টুলস:

Termux – Lab scripting & automation

Android Emulator – Lab testing of email or messaging apps


সফটওয়্যার প্যাকেজ:

Python: smtplib, requests – Automated lab email/message delivery

Node.js / JavaScript – Lab portal & message simulation

SQLite / DB tools – Logging & analysis


হার্ডওয়্যার / ল্যাব সেটআপ:

Lab PC or server for running simulations & VMs

Isolated lab network for safe testing

Optional VoIP or messaging simulators for lab-only interaction
